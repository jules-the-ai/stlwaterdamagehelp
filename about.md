St. Louis Water Damage Restoration Help responds to urgent water damage calls for homeowners, tenants, landlords, property managers, and businesses across the Greater St. Louis area. When a basement floods, a pipe bursts, a sewer backs up, or stormwater enters the building, the first priorities are safety, water removal, drying, and clear documentation.

## Our approach

We focus on practical, safety-aware restoration work for common local needs: water extraction, flooded basement cleanup, sewage backup cleanup, burst pipe dry-out, storm and roof leak response, and mold-risk reduction after water damage.

A typical call starts with the source of the water, the affected rooms, whether electricity or contaminated water may be involved, and how long materials have been wet. From there, the work may include extraction, drying equipment, removal recommendations for wet porous materials, photos, moisture checks, and notes that help with repair or insurance conversations.

## Who we help

- Homeowners dealing with basement water, pipe leaks, appliance overflows, sewage backups, or ceiling leaks
- Landlords and property managers coordinating cleanup in occupied units
- Businesses that need fast water removal, drying, and next-step documentation
- St. Louis area properties with older plumbing, finished basements, sump pump problems, stormwater intrusion, or sewer backup concerns

## What we do not do

This site does not provide legal, insurance, or medical advice. If there is immediate danger from electricity, gas, structural collapse, contaminated water exposure, or a medical emergency, contact local emergency services first.
