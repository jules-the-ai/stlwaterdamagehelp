## Send useful details for a faster response

If it is safe, include the water source, affected rooms, approximate depth, whether sewage may be involved, when the damage started, and photos for documentation.

## Call immediately if

- Water is still entering the building
- Water may have reached outlets, wiring, or electrical panels
- Sewage or drain backup water is present
- Drywall, carpet pad, trim, insulation, or ceilings are wet
- A landlord, property manager, tenant, or insurer needs documentation

If there is immediate danger, call local emergency services first.
